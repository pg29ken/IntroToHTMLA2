Play Minesweeper!

How To Play

Objective

Reveal all the mines without triggering them

Controls

Left Click: Reveals a tile

Right Click: Flag a tile

Tile Types

Safe tiles: Have numbers indicating the number of mines adjacent to them

Mines: End the game once revealed

How to Win

Correctly flag all potential mines
